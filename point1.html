Key Characteristics of Anime

Art Style: Features distinct visual traits like large, expressive eyes, vibrant hair colors, and dynamic action framing.

Not a Genre: Anime is an art form and a medium. It spans countless genres, from horror and romance to science fiction and historical drama.

Demographics: Unlike western animation, which is largely aimed at kids, anime has specific demographic categories: Kodomomuke, Shonen, Shojo, Seinen/Josei.

Popular Platforms: You can stream official releases on Crunchyroll or Netflix.
